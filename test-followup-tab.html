Follow-up System Overview

The Enhanced Consultation V2 now includes two follow-up related tabs:

  • Follow-Up Plan - The form for creating follow-up appointments and instructions (FollowUpTab.tsx)
  • Follow-Up Overview - Read-only view of completed appointments with follow-up forms (FollowUpOverviewTab.tsx)

How to Use Follow-up Functionality

1. During Consultation (Follow-Up Plan Tab)

  • Open the Enhanced Consultation V2
  • Navigate to the "Follow-Up Plan" tab
  • You can:
    • Schedule general follow-up appointments
    • Add tooth-specific follow-up appointments
    • Set post-care instructions
    • Record follow-up evaluations
    • Request appointments (3m, 6m)

2. After Appointment Completion (Follow-Up Overview Tab)

  • Navigate to the "Follow-Up Overview" tab
  • You'll see all completed appointments
  • Click the "Follow-up" checkbox next to a completed appointment
  • Fill out the follow-up form that appears

✅ Both tabs are now available in Enhanced Consultation V2

The navigation order is:

  1. Chief Complaint
  2. HOPI
  3. Medical History
  4. Personal History
  5. Clinical Examination
  6. Investigations
  7. Clinical Diagnosis
  8. Treatment Plan
  9. Prescription
  10. Follow-Up Plan (NEW - The form)
  11. Follow-Up Overview (View appointments)
  12. Live Diagnosis Overview
  13. Live Treatment Overview

Follow-up Tab Features

Previous Follow-ups Section

  • Shows history of all follow-ups across consultations
  • Filter by tooth number or type
  • Quick request buttons (3m, 6m) for each tooth with treatments

Tooth-Specific Follow-up

  • Interactive tooth map (click to select)
  • Add follow-up appointments for specific teeth
  • Links to tooth diagnosis automatically

Record Follow-up (Encounter)

  • Record patient's current status
  • Track symptoms (improved/same/worsened)
  • Pain score, swelling, bleeding, etc.
  • Wound and suture status
  • Medication adherence

General Follow-up Appointments

  • Schedule appointments not tied to specific teeth
  • Quick type buttons (Healing Check, Suture Removal, etc.)
  • Priority levels (low/medium/high/urgent)

⚠️ Note

The follow-up data is saved as part of the consultation. Remember to save the consultation draft or complete it to persist your follow-up plans.
